Lohikeitto

traditional Finnish salmon soup, easy to make and taste heavenly.



Ingredients


  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 pound potatoes(cubed)
  • 1 carrot(sliced)
  • 1 pound salmon(cut into large pieces
  • 1 cup 35% cream
  • salt and black pepper(to taste)
  • Fresh dill(chopped)


  1. Heat butter in a large saucepan over medium-high heat; add leeks and cook stirring for 5-6 minutes or until leek is welted.

  2. Add cubed potatoes, carrot and fish stock.

  3. Bring to a boil and let vegetable cook for 10-15 minutes depend on the size of the vegetable.

  4. Add salmon and cream and simmer for 5-6 minutes, adjust seasoning.

  5. Sprinkle with chopped dill and serve.
